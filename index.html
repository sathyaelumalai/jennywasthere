Mission

The Clinical-Operational Bridge

Innovation is only as good as its execution at the bedside. I build the systems that make detection a reality.

Coming from a nursing background, I saw firsthand how patients are often lost in the gaps between data and action. Aidar is where we close those gaps.

i. Care

The patient is more than a data point.

In my years as an RN, I learned that technology must serve the person, not the other way around. My role as COO is to ensure every deployment respects the clinical workflow and the patient's dignity.

ii. Systems

Efficiency is a clinical necessity.

With an MBA lens, I translate clinical needs into operational protocols. We don't just ship hardware; we ship workflows that save time and save lives.

iii. Growth

Scaling compassion through technology.

Scaling a medical device company requires a unique blend of regulatory discipline and clinical empathy. We are building the operational backbone for the future of chronic care.

Execution is the heart of innovation.

— Journey

From Bedside to Boardroom.

Two decades of bridging the gap between clinical care and operational excellence.

Foundation · 2005-2015

Clinical Nursing & Education.

Registered Nurse (RN) background with a focus on acute and chronic care. MSN in Nursing Leadership, followed by an MBA to master the business of healthcare. A career built on understanding the patient's journey firsthand.

Inception · 2016-2021

Co-Founding Aidar Health.

Co-founded Aidar Health with a mission to simplify chronic care. Led the operational path to FDA 510(k) clearance for MouthLab and established the company's clinical research protocols.

Scale · 2022-Present

Operational Excellence at Scale.

Overseeing the national rollout of MouthLab across 10+ long-term care facilities. Leading clinical studies on Long COVID and pioneering the integration of AIDI™ into physician workflows.
